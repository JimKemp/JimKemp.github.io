The Raft

The greatest vessel to ever grace the waters of New Hampshire

Skills Used
Goofing Around: Woodworking Electrical Wiring Deepened Understanding of Heat Dissipation

The story begins with a close friend of mine who is also an Eagle Scout. I spend many weekends visiting him in New Hampsire, hiking in out-of-the-way places, swimming in lakes. Several years ago, we were paddleboarding around a lake when we noticed the entire Nothern shore was uninhabited. In fact, it was a nature preserve, although an extremely remote and undervisited one. No trail in the preserve lead to this rugged shoreline, and the ground was so rocky and uneven that picthing a tent would be almost impossble.

But we were undeterred. If we could find a clearing, the ideal solution would be to construct a platform, making the ground pleasant to sleep on. I deployed a drone to scout out the area and identified three potential sites, then made the jourey by paddleboard to confirm the dimensons of the largest clearing From there the specifications of the platform were well-defined - but how to get it to the side?

It would have to be by water
