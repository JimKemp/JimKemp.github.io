Engineering Consulting for FLX Solutions

Skills Used
Mech: Solidworks CAD 3D Printing PCB Design and Manufacture
Electrical: Surface-Mount Soldering Use of a pick-and-place machine
Software: Python Rust
Teamwork: Experience in a growing startup Management of a laboratory environment

I work part time during the academic year for FLX solutions, a company developing snake-like robotic inspection technology for hard-to-reach spaces. I have two primary roles at FLX: I assist the mechatronics lead in designing, manufacturing and testing components, and I'm working on a longer-term reaserch project developing a mobile robot base that could deploy FLX's technology without a human present. In the course of assisting the mechatronics team, I am responsible for the operation of FLX's lab and manufacturing facility at Ben Franklin Techn Ventures, a startup accelerator. Running the lab has refined my skills working with the 3D printers, surface-mount reflow ovens, pick-and-place machines, and shop tools that FLX needs for well-built prototypes.

Working for FLX continues to expose me to every apect of a robotics company, as their small size means I get the chance to help out with all aspects of the technology and business. I'm excited to continue working with FLX to expand inspection robotics into new fields.
